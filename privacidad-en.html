By providing your personal data through this website's form and submitting your request, you acknowledge that you have read this Privacy Notice and grant your consent for the processing of your data according to the purposes described herein. 5.Transfers of Personal Data

The Controller will not transfer your personal data to third parties without your consent, except in the cases permitted by law, such as when the transfer is established by regulation, is necessary for the administration of justice, or takes place between companies of the same corporate group under common policies.

Should the Controller need to transfer data to third parties beyond those cases, you will be informed and, whenever legally required, your consent will be obtained.

6.Means to Exercise ARCO Rights and Revoke Consent

You have the right to know what personal data we hold about you and how we use it (Access); to request correction when it is inaccurate or incomplete (Rectification); to request deletion from our records when you believe it is not being used in compliance with the law (Cancellation); and to object to the use of your data for specific purposes (Opposition). These are known as ARCO rights.

To exercise any of these rights, or to revoke your consent, please send a request to info@orbitjk.com indicating:

  • Name of the data subject and a means to communicate the response.
  • Document proving identity or legal representation, if applicable.
  • Clear and precise description of the right you wish to exercise or the specific request.
  • Any element that facilitates the location of the personal data.

The Controller will respond to your request within the time periods established by applicable law.

7.Means to Limit the Use or Disclosure of Your Data

You may request limitation of the use or disclosure of your personal data by sending your request to info@orbitjk.com. Likewise, any communication you receive from us will include a mechanism allowing you to request that no further communications be sent.

8.Use of Cookies and Tracking Technologies

This website does not use cookies or tracking technologies that automatically collect personal data.

9.Changes to the Privacy Notice

This Privacy Notice may be modified due to legal changes, internal practice updates or new requirements. Any change will be published on this same page, indicating the date of the latest update. We recommend reviewing it periodically.

10.Data Protection Authority

If you believe your right to personal data protection has been violated, you may turn to the competent authority in Mexico regarding the protection of personal data held by private parties.
