Privacy Notice

1.Identity and Address of the Controller

This website corresponds to the ORBIT JK project, operated from Puebla, Mexico, and is responsible for the processing of the personal data you provide through the contact channels published on this website.

For purposes of this notice, ORBIT JK acts as controller with respect to the information collected for contact, technical evaluation, commercial follow-up and coordination of specialized conversations linked to the project.

2.Personal Data We Collect

Through the contact form available on the website, ORBIT JK may collect the following personal data directly:

  • Name.
  • Corporate email address.
  • Organization or company.
  • Professional role or function.
  • Primary interest stated in the form.
  • Any additional information you voluntarily include in your message.

ORBIT JK does not request or seek to collect sensitive, financial or asset-related personal data through this website.

3.Purposes of Processing

Primary purposes

Your personal data is used for the necessary purposes that give rise to the contact relationship with you:

  • To handle requests for technical evaluation, whitepaper access or complementary information.
  • To understand the institutional and operational context of the interested organization.
  • To follow up on conversations about orbital intelligence, validation, integration or strategic collaboration.
  • To coordinate meetings, calls or subsequent information exchanges.
  • To protect the operational integrity of the project and filter requests clearly unrelated to the website's purpose.

Secondary purposes

Additionally, ORBIT JK may use your data to share institutional updates, related technical documents or new outreach opportunities with organizations that have previously shown interest, provided such communications maintain a reasonable connection to the original request.

If you do not want your data to be processed for these secondary purposes, you may request it under section 7 of this notice.

4.Consent

By providing your personal data through the website form and voluntarily sending your request, you acknowledge that you have read this Privacy Notice and authorize the processing of your data under the terms described herein.

You may revoke your consent at any time to the extent that doing so does not prevent compliance with legal obligations or the retention of records necessary to document prior communications.

5.Transfers of Personal Data

ORBIT JK does not market or transfer your personal data to third parties for purposes unrelated to the request you submitted.

Your data may only be shared when there is a legal obligation, an order from a competent authority, or when it is strictly necessary for the technical or operational support of the website under reasonable confidentiality conditions.

6.Means to Exercise ARCO Rights

You have the right to Access your personal data, Rectify it if inaccurate, Cancel it when you consider it no longer necessary for the informed purposes, and Oppose its processing for specific purposes.

To exercise your ARCO rights, you may submit a request through the contact form available in the contact section, clearly indicating:

  • Your name and a means to communicate the response.
  • The right you wish to exercise.
  • Data or background information that helps locate your prior request.
  • Any clarification that facilitates handling your request.

ORBIT JK will review each request and respond within a reasonable period according to applicable regulations and the nature of the received requirement.

7.Means to Limit the Use or Disclosure of Your Data

If you wish to limit the use or disclosure of your personal data, you may request it through the same contact form on the website, expressly indicating that you want to stop receiving subsequent communications or that your information should not be used for secondary purposes.

8.Use of Cookies and Tracking Technologies

At the time of publication of this notice, ORBIT JK does not use advertising cookies, automated profiles or tracking technologies aimed at collecting personal data for commercial segmentation purposes within this informational website.

If analytics, automation or measurement tools that involve additional data processing are incorporated in the future, this notice will be updated accordingly.

9.Changes to the Privacy Notice

ORBIT JK may update this Privacy Notice as a result of legal changes, operational adjustments to the website or new information-processing needs.

Any modification will be published on this same page, so we recommend reviewing it periodically before submitting information through the form.

10.Contact and Data Protection Authority

If you consider that your right to personal data protection has been violated, you may contact the competent authority in Mexico regarding personal data protection.

Questions about this notice

For questions related to this Privacy Notice or the processing of your data, use the form available in the contact section or the contact channels published on the main site.

This document is drafted for the informational and operational use of the ORBIT JK site. If the project adopts a specific legal entity or integrates new data-capture platforms, it is advisable to update this notice to reflect those changes with greater legal detail.

If you need to exercise a right or make an inquiry, use the official site channel

The main form allows requests related to privacy, technical evaluation and institutional follow-up to be routed without leaving the ORBIT JK ecosystem.
